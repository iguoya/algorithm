ASL = \sum_{i=1}^n C_iP_i \ ASL = \sum_{i=1}^n n \times \frac{1}{n} = \frac{n+1}{2} \ ASL = \frac{n/2+1}{2} + \sum_{i=1}^{n/2} n \times 1/2 = \frac{n/2+1}{2} + n/2
